The AHFAD Shehata History

More then 30 years of experience

Thirty years ago, Mr. Shehata decided to open a small restaurant in one of Cairo’s busy streets, with a simple goal: to serve authentic Egyptian food with a true homemade taste. The restaurant started with just a few pans and a handful of tables, but thanks to his skill and commitment to quality, people quickly began talking about it.

Over the years, AHFAD Shehata became one of the most well-known spots in the area, famous for Shehata’s special seasoning and a flavor that has never changed since day one. And today, after 30 years of experience, the restaurant still holds the same principle: “Authentic flavor… the secret of Shehata.”
